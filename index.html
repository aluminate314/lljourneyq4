Our Journey




Our YouTube channel was first established on Nov. 17, 2022. It was a group
decision to choose a podcast as our category as the idea of it appealed to
us the most. From then on, we brainstormed for our branding, our intro video,
and our future possible topics.




A month after the creation of our channel (on Dec. 9), we launched our first
video which was our short intro video that we will be using at the beginning
of all our videos as much as possible.




Then on Mar. 17, 2023, we launched our second video which focused more on
introducing what our channel was all about. Although it seems like a short
and simple video, we planned and thought about its contents carefully and
created a storyboard for it ot remain organized.

This video included our mission, goals, what we do, etc.




For our last school quarter, we decided to post a podcast to start off our
podcasting career. We chose the topic of digital art tips as everyone in
our group has had some experience with it, thus making it easier to give
tips from the heart.




Throughout the quarter, we were able to advertise our brand through posting
in our social media that we created after posting our second video.




We can say that our advertising were effective for we observed an increase in
subscribers and in views, amassing a total of 160+ views based from our
statistics.
